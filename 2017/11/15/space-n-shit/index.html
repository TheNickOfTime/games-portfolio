Space n’ Shit

NickNovember 15, 2017March 22, 2018

This is another 2 week design project. The goal was to create a twin-stick-shooter type game that had some sort of twist in the gameplay and ‘story’ of it. The story side of things is that you play as a small star who wants to become a big star. You float around outer space, picking up colorful bits of space dust, growing bigger and bigger, until you become so big that you implode and become a black hole. On the gameplay side of things, rather than shooting projectiles, the player controls a large solar flare that the star shoots out. This is primarily used as a tentacle of sorts to gather in more bits of space dust but is also your first defense against other stars and planets that come crashing at you, once you gain a large enough gravitational force. Creating the solar flare was a challenge, but ultimately the time was worth it, as it feels very nice to control and adds a ton of novelty to playing this minigame. Overall this was a fun project that ended up being a good split of design and technical focus.
