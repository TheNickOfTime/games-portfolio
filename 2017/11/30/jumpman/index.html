Jumpman

NickNovember 30, 2017March 22, 2018

I was tasked with taking a mechanic from any game and replicating it at its most basic level of functionality, where it is still fun. Having just played Super Mario Odyssey, I was interested in replicating some of Mario’s movement mechanics. I did not have the time to replicate the signature ‘Cappy throw’ from the game, but I was able to replicate his famous triple jump, crouch jump, ground pound, and ground pound jump. From a design perspective, these moves all have unique situations where they aid in Mario’s mobility. This was a fun project that forced me to simplify the tech side of things and focus on fine tuning numbers on the design side of things to get it feeling just right. While there is room for improvement, I am quite happy with completing this in just a few hours, as I changed my mind on this assignment last minute. I recommend playing it, it is actually a fun little sample level to jump around in!
