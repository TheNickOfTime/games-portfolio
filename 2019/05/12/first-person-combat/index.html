First Person Combat

NickMay 12, 2019May 20, 2019

I have recently been working in Unreal 4 to make a first-person combat focused game. Dishonored was the primary inspiration for the base format, but I want the gameplay to have less focus on stealth. Additionally, I wanted it to be a situation of Swords (the player) vs. Guns (the enemy). This will require the player to be quick on their feet and use their arsenal in the most effective way.

I have only been working on it for a few weeks at this point, but I have the basic player actions and basic enemy AI implemented at this point. Take a look below and keep an eye out for more updates!
