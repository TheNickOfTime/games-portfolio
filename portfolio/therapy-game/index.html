Therapy Game

Category Archive, Design Challenge, Pitch

This is a pitch for an AR based physical therapy game for children with cerebral palsy. This was an assignment for a class where we were prompted to come up with the concept for a game that could help children who are sick or disabled in some way. In addition to that, our client was Samsung, and we were requested to impliment AR technology found in samsung phones, and use projection mapping technology in some manner. It was decided in class that projection mapping portions would be unreasonable to develop in the imagined timeframe.

This assignment was an in class competition, and the winner would have their idea turned into a collaborative project over the summer. Technically our pitch recieved second place, but it was more or less the AR portion of our game that was actually used for the project. I did not participate in the actual development of this project, to be clear.
